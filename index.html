Fantasy+ Football: The Playbook

DRAFT. DOMINATE. WIN.

Welcome to Fantasy+, your all-in-one fantasy sports command center. Leave the boring ten-man backyard setups and spreadsheets in the past. You are now the GM. This is your blueprint to absolute gridiron supremacy.

1. THE ARENA: TWO WAYS TO WIN

We don’t do standard utility apps, and we don't do boring restrictions. Choose your battleground—or dominate both:

  • The Fantasy Division (The Ultimate Roster): Draft an elite 11-player powerhouse squad every single week. No broken draft boards, no limiting salary caps—just pure performance. High score rules the week.
  • The Picks Division (Predictive Prowess): Lock in up to 5 "More/Less" player predictions each week. Put your football IQ to the test and watch the board light up.

The Big Picture: Every weekly score stacks up for a massive, season-long campaign. Go for the weekly crown or play the long game for ultimate legendary status.

2. GETTING STARTED: COMMAND YOUR LEAGUE

Ready to deploy? Setting up takes seconds:

  • Launch a Private League: Challenge your friends and secure the ultimate bragging rights.
  • Join the Ranks: Step into public leagues to trade blows with die-hard fans who actually know ball.
  • The National Tournament: Take it to the grandest stage possible and compete against every single player on the app.

3. FANTASY DIVISION: THE 11-MAN POWERHOUSE

Every week is a clean slate. You build your lineup exactly how you want it from our curated player pools.

Your Weekly Lineup:

  • Quarterback (QB): 2 Slots
  • Running Back (RB): 2 Slots
  • Tight End (TE): 1 Slot
  • Flex (FLX): 2 Slots (RB/WR/TE)
  • Kicker (K): 1 Slot
  • Defensive End (DE): 1 Slot

Absolute Roster Freedom

  • Pick Whoever, Whenever: No traditional draft restrictions holding you back. Select your weapons from the weekly position pools.
  • Live Edits on the Fly: Make roster adjustments right up until each individual player’s game kicks off. Real-time stats keep you sharp.

4. PICKS DIVISION: HIGH-STAKES PREDICTIONS

Think you can predict the future? Prove it.

  • The Play: Browse the "Team" or "Players" sections and check the projected scores.
  • The Call: Tap the arrows to lock in MORE or LESS for 1 to 5 players each week.
  • The Deadline: Lock it in before kickoff. Once the game starts, the vault is closed.

5. THE LEADERBOARD & THE GLORY

Track your rise in real time with sleek, dynamic leaderboards built for individual leagues and the national stage.

Crowning the Champions

  • Weekly Contests: Highest fantasy points wins the Fantasy Division title; most correct predictions takes the Picks Division crown.
  • Season-Long Campaign: True dominance requires consistency. Your scores accumulate all season long to crown the undisputed Season-Long Champions.

The Tie-Breakers (Survival of the Fittest)

If a battlefield ends in a draw, we break the tie using this strict order of operations:

  1. Score in the opposite division for that week.
  2. Season-long score for that division.
  3. Season-long score for the opposite division.
  4. Prior week's score for that division.
  5. Prior week's score for the opposite division.
  6. Current week’s highest-scoring individual player.
  7. Alphabetical username supremacy (pick a good name).

6. EXTRA INCENTIVES: THE DROPS

The tournament organizer loves a winner. At our absolute discretion, we unleash premium prize drops to top performers and threshold-breakers.

  • The Loot: Nominal rewards, sponsor discounts, subscriptions, and gift cards.
  • The Delivery: Boom. Sent directly via SMS text to your verified account phone number.

7. GM REMINDERS

  • Deadlines are Lethal: Submissions lock player-by-player as their games kick off. Don't get caught sleeping.
  • The Final Whistle: Winners are locked after Monday Night Football. Late stat corrections won't change history.
  • Stay Sharp: Check the injury reports, monitor the matchups, and play with swagger.
